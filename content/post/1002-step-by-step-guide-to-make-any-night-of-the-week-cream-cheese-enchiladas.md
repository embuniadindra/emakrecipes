##### Steps to make Cream Cheese Enchiladas:

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
1. Boil chicken until done and flavor that broth!! (I add one whole onion, garlic, jalape√±os, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, &amp; knorr tomato bouillon - I use a scoop of broth for the mixture, also for my Mexican rice on the side)
1. Place chicken in separate bowl. shred it. add mixture ingredients: Cream cheese, cheese, green chiles, rotel tomatoes, &amp; I add about 1/3 cup of the broth to the mixture (optional). Mix well.
