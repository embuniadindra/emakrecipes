##### The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread for a crowd:

1. Take 4 eggs
1. Take 1 tsp vanilla
1. Get 2 1/2 tsp baking soda
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
1. Get 3 1/2 cup all purpose flour
