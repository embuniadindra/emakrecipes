##### The ingredients needed to make Tiramisu Chocolate Yogurt:

1. Get as needed Rusk or Bread toast/Finger Biscuits
1. Prepare 2 Spoon Coffee
1. Prepare 2 Cup Milk
1. Take 1 Cup Powdered Sugar
1. Get 2 Cup Yogurt
1. Get 1 Cup Fresh Cream
1. Take 1 Spoon Vanilla Essence
1. Prepare as needed Chopped Chocolate/Chocolate Chips
1. Prepare 1 Spoon Cocoa Powder
1. Make ready 1 Spoon Sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Tiramisu Chocolate Yogurt:

1. Firstly take a Container add 2 Cup full Cream Milk then mix 2 Spoon full Coffee and Sugar stir well and make Coffee mixture Dip for Rusk or Biscuits and keep aside.
1. In another Bowl add 2 Cup full Yogurt and 1 Cup Powder Sugar blend it and make a smooth paste near about 5-7 min.
1. Now add Fresh Cream 1 Cup, Vanilla Essence into it 1 Spoon and blend it to make a smooth Cream yogurt paste.
1. Afterwards Take a Cake Pan put Rusk or Biscuits into it, Dip or Spread Coffee mixture over it.
1. Now cover the biscuits with Cream yogurt Paste over the biscuits then again spread the layer of biscuits then spread Cream yogurt Paste over it.
1. Now Sprinkle over cocoa powder and Chopped Chocolate over it then keep it in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours for Deep freeze.
1. After 2 hours cut into pieces serve cool brew TIRAMISU Italian Dessert is Ready to Enjoy.
