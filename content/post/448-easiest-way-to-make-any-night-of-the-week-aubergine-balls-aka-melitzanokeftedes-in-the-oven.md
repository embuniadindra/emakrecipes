1. Make ready 1 onion medium size
1. Get 50 gr hard cheese like parmesan
1. Get 30 ml olive oil
1. Make ready  Salt and pepper
1. Make ready 150 gr rye rusk or bread crumbs
1. Prepare 1 egg
