##### The ingredients needed to make Twice Baked Sweet Potato:

1. Get 2 big sweet potato
1. Get 2 stalks spring onion (chop finely)
1. Make ready 2 cubed unsalted butter
1. Prepare 2 garlic (chopped)
1. Get  Baby spinach
1. Prepare  Grated cheese (mozzarella/ cheddar)
1. Get  Grated parmesan cheese
1. Make ready 2 tbsp cooking cream
1. Prepare  Black pepper
1. Get  Olive oil
1. Make ready  Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Twice Baked Sweet Potato:

1. Preheat oven 180°C / 350°F
1. Put sweet potato in baking dish, completely coat in oil &amp; bake for 60 mins
1. Heat up the pan,add butter,minced garlic, baby spinach &amp; chopped spring onion. Stir for 30 seconds
1. Transfer cooked baby spinach to mixing bowl. Let cool down
1. Once cool,add the cream, salt,black pepper, grated parmesan cheese
1. Remove sweet potatoes once cooked.
1. Slice in to half,leave it to cool for 10 mins. Scoop out the flesh, add to mixing bowl together with spinach.
1. Stir the flesh &amp; baby spinach till mix well
1. Fill the sweet potato skin
1. Spread the top with grated mozzarella / cheddar cheese
1. Bake for 15 - 20 mins at 180°C / 350°F
1. After 15-20 mins, take it out, leave to cool for 10 mins
1. Transfer to serving dish &amp; spread some chopped parsley
