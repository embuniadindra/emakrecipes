##### The ingredients needed to make Minestrone soup:

1. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
1. Take 1 large onion, chopped
1. Prepare 2 clove garlic, chopped
1. Prepare 1 large potato, diced to cubes
1. Prepare 1 large carrot, chopped
1. Get 2 medium celery
1. Make ready 1 large tomato, chopped
1. Make ready 1 tbsp tomato paste
1. Take 3 tbsp kidney beans
1. Take 3 tbsp hummus
1. Prepare 6 cup chicken broth
1. Prepare 1 cup pasta
1. Prepare 1 tbsp basil
1. Make ready 1 parmesan cheese
1. Take 1 salt
1. Get 1 black pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Minestrone soup:

1. Heat the olive oil and butter in a large pot over medium-high heat.
1. Add the onion and cook until translucent, about 4 min. Then Add the garlic and cook 30 seconds.
1. Add the celery and carrot and cook until they begin to soften, about 5 min. Stir in the basil, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and pepper to taste; cook 3 more minutes.
1. Add the diced tomatoes &amp; paste + chicken broth to the pot and bring to a boil.
1. Reduce the heat to medium low and simmer 10 minutes.
1. Stir in the hummus, potato, kidney beans &amp; pasta and cook until the pasta and vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes.
1. Ladle into bowls and top with the parmesan and chopped basil.
