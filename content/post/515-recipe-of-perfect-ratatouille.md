##### The ingredients needed to make Ratatouille:

1. Get 1/2 large Tomato
1. Prepare 1 medium Onion
1. Take 1/2 medium Carrot
1. Make ready 1 medium Potatoes
1. Make ready 1 medium Eggplant - small Japanese type
1. Get 1 medium Green peppers
1. Get 1 Other vegetables of your choice
1. Take 1 clove Garlic (finely chopped)
1. Take 200 ml Tomato juice or tomato puree
1. Make ready 1 Salt and pepper
1. Get 1 Red chili pepper
1. Take 2 to 3 tablespoons Olive oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Ratatouille:

1. Cut all the vegetables into same-sized cubes.
1. Put a generous amount of olive oil (2-3 tablespoons) in a frying pan. Add the garlic and red chili pepper and fry until the flavours develop.
1. Start frying the vegetables, starting with the firm ones and adding the softer ones as you go.
1. When they are mostly cooked through, season with salt and pepper.
1. Add the tomato juice or tomato puree and simmer. Simmer over a low heat until there is not much liquid left in the pan.
1. It&#39;s done!! It&#39;s easy but delicious . Serve it on a piece of baguette. Bon Appétit!!
1. If you want to sweeten it, add ketchup or Worcestershire-type sauce to taste!
