##### The ingredients needed to make Traditional Italian carbonara:

1. Take 70 gr guanciale or Italian pancetta
1. Get 1 egg
1. Prepare  Salt and pepper
1. Prepare  Pecorino or parmigiano

##### Steps to make Traditional Italian carbonara:

1. ▪️brown the guanciale on it’s own
1. ▪️make in a small bowl a sauce with the egg,a bit of pepper and parmigiano
1. ▪️cook the pasta al dente(9 minutes not more)
1. ▪️when is cooked just mix on a high heat with guanciale ▪️move the pan from the heat and add the egg ▪️just mix it very quickly and make sure that the egg is not cooked (it has to be a cream) ▪️serve the pasta with a bit of pepper on top and parmigiano
