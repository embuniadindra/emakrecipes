##### The ingredients needed to make Heidi&#39;s buttery mint potatoes:

1. Get 16 or more cocktail/baby potatoes
1. Make ready 3 or more heaped tbs butter
1. Get 3 or more tbs thick mint sauce
1. Get 1 pinch sea salt

##### Steps to make Heidi&#39;s buttery mint potatoes:

1. In a pot of boiling salted water, cook the cocktail potatoes until tender
1. Drain potatoes and place in a large bowl. Add the salt, butter and thick mint sauce whilst the potatoes are still warm so the butter melts easily
1. Stir potatoes through the butter mixture and taste. Add more butter if you like a buttery taste or more mint sauce if you prefer more tang
1. Serve as a yummy side to steak, chicken, fish or Lamb. This can be eaten hot or cold
